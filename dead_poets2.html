DEAD POETS II

A POEM

by
The Atavistic Bunny

Love is Just a Word

Have you ever put your


all into

A relationship that’s

not healthy?



Wrote a thousand

love letters,

gone on a thousand dates,


Bought a thousand flowers,

and still not

feel it’s enough.

Have you ever overlooked every


,flaw all in the

name of love?

To smile in the eye of the


storm even as it tears you apart.

Worried and

confused because


you’re not yet willing

to depart.

Give excuses for

every vile action and


hurtful word that

hurts

your heart.

I know it’s

hard to believe,


but love is not enough.

Enough to

compromise your

dreams and hopes.


Enough to stand

in the background


of your romantic

love scene.

Watching your ,

life flash before


you like it’s

all just a dream.

Sometimes love is just a word


A word we use to mask the pain


we’re not willing to overcome.

The pain we’re


not ready for because

we know it’s yet to come.

A feeling so familiar


but new at the same time.

The heartbreak we endure


that’s not even worth a dime.

Love is a word we use when .


we’re not ready to let go

You say ‘I love

her’ but she’s


overstepping your

boundaries.

She says ‘I love him’

but she’s


just tolerating his

immaturity.

Times we overlook that

healthy.


relationship for a touch
of

familiarity.

Love is just a word.


Take a deep breathe.


Open your eyes and don’t

their flaws trap you like a bird.
