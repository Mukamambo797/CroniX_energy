About us

Embark on a Journey of Knowledge: CBU, Zambia's Academic Beacon

Welcome to CBU CroniX, the E-magazine crafted by and for the vibrant student community of Copperbelt University (CBU)! CBU, nestled in the heart of Kitwe, is Zambia's premier academic institution, a shining beacon of knowledge in the bustling Copperbelt region.

Born in 1972 as the Zambia Institute of Technology (ZIT), CBU's early mission was to forge the skilled workforce needed for the booming mining industry. ZIT meticulously trained technicians in fields like Electrical, Electronics, and Mining, fueling the engines of growth in Zambia's mineral-rich heartland.

1978 marked a historic turning point. The University of Zambia Ndola (UNZADO) merged with ZIT, culminating in the birth of the vibrant academic powerhouse we know today as Copperbelt University.

At CBU, academic excellence flourishes across a diverse spectrum of disciplines, from the humanities to the cutting edge of engineering. Our modern campus, brimming with cutting-edge facilities and a dynamic community, fosters an environment where students blossom both intellectually and personally. CBU's rich history is intricately woven into the fabric of Zambia's progress. From humble beginnings, the university has blossomed into a powerhouse of knowledge and research, shaping the nation's educational landscape and fueling its future.

Driven by a steadfast commitment to delivering top-tier education and nurturing skilled graduates, CBU CroniX invites you to dive into the captivating world of CBU. Stay connected as we share captivating narratives, insightful perspectives, and up-to-date news that pulsate from the heart of campus and beyond. CBU CroniX is, at its core, a student-driven initiative, dedicated to inspiring and informing the CBU community. The information we present is meticulously sourced from trusted and authentic channels. Sprinkled throughout are thought-provoking quotes to ignite your cognitive spark, and carefully chosen scriptures to illuminate your soul.

Embark on a journey of intellectual exploration with CBU CroniX. Welcome to the vibrant community shaping Zambia's future!
