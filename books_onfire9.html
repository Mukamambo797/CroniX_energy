THE TIME TRAVELLER FROM THE EAST

Zara Chabby found himself surrounded by the fervor emanating from his disciples, whose excitement mirrored the widespread recognition he had gained across the globe.

Regardless of his whereabouts, crowds gathered, eager to absorb his wisdom. The burden of this acclaim, however, became oppressive, propelling Zara Chabby to seek solace in the expansive solitude of the desert—an environment he held dear, a solitude he yearned for.

Committed to maintaining silence during this desert sojourn, he roamed aimlessly through the ever-shifting sands.

Nights came and went in the timeless expanse of the desert. Seeking refuge beneath cautious shade during scorching days and under the moon's brilliance at night, Zara Chabby sustained himself not with food but with the ceaseless thoughts that raced through his mind.

On the fifth day, seated beneath the sheltering catai shade, a peculiar man approached.

This stranger appeared out of sync with the contemporary era. Impeccably groomed and carrying an air of sophistication, he bore a briefcase and surveyed his surroundings with an air of detachment.

Glancing at his wristwatch, the man approached Zara Chabby and, without a word, seated himself beside the silent sage.

Zara Chabby:

(Inwardly sensing a unique presence)

Greetings, traveler of time. Your arrival in the solitude of the desert implies a purpose beyond the bounds of our earthly timeline.

Time Traveler:

(Smirking knowingly)

Indeed, Zara Chabby. I traverse the ages, observing the intricate dance of existence. Time, a vast tapestry of woven moments, holds untold tales.

Zara Chabby:

(Curious)

What urgency drives you in the eternal vastness? What compels you to move with such haste?

Time Traveler:

(With a flicker of amusement)

Haste, my friend, is but a relative concept. In the currents of time, urgency takes varied forms. My mission spans epochs, and each moment lost may reverberate through eternity.

Zara Chabby:

(Acknowledging the complexity)

Time, the silent orchestrator of destinies. But do you walk the predetermined path of destiny, or do you possess the power to alter the cosmic currents?

Time Traveler:

(Eyes gleaming mysteriously)

Ah, the delicate ballet of fate and free will. Each step I take resonates through the fabric of time, a dance where causality and choice entwine.

Zara chabby:

(Reflective)

A dance indeed. Yet, do you not fear disrupting the delicate balance? What of the consequences that may arise from meddling with the threads

Time Traveler:

(With a cryptic smile)

Fear, dear sage, is but a shadow cast by the unknown. I embrace uncertainty, for within it lies the essence of discovery.

Zara Chabby:

(Contemplative)

Discovery, the force that propels us forward. Tell me, traveler, what profound wisdom have you gleaned from traversing the epochs?

Time Traveler:

(Gazing into the horizon)

Wisdom, like grains of sand slipping through my fingers in this vast desert. Yet, I've learned that the beauty of existence lies not in the destination but in the journey itself.

Zara Chabby:

(Smiling knowingly)

Profound indeed. As we sit beneath the cosmic expanse, let our words echo through time, carrying the essence of this encounter to the ears of future wanderers.

Time Traveler:

(Rising)

Time beckons, Zara Chabby. Our conversation, a fleeting moment in its eternal dance. Until our paths intertwine again, across the ages.

With those enigmatic words, the time traveler dissolved into the folds of time, leaving Zara Chabby to contemplate the mysteries of existence beneath the silent embrace of the desert night. How Zara Chabby sensed the time traveler's unique aura remained an unspoken mystery, a silent acknowledgment between two beings tuned to the enigmatic rhythms of time.

But times had channged and the Time Traveller needs a new watch.
