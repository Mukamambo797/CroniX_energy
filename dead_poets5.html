Echoes in the Silence

In the symphony of silence,

echoes unfold,

Whispers and secrets,

stories untold.

Words dance, a

captivating sensation,

Crafted illusions,

a language's flirtation.

Wrapped in enigma,

a linguistic art,

My ears perceive,

but it's just the start.

Words, weightless

but mighty,

A gateway to emotions,

a clandestine

flighty.

Unravel the mystery,

let meanings transcend,

Language, a canvas, where

interpretations blend.

In the silence,

where emotions create,

Words echo loudly,

sealing fate.

Ipilwe ya Musonda (Musonda's Moon)

Mwaka wa lufubu, insoni shaba,

Musonda akwata ici luna mu nshitima.

Ubushiku bwakosa, inyimbo shilala,

Ipilwe yakwe yalenga mutima wakwe.

Umwana wamwaka, amenso yakwe ayalenga,

Ipilwe ya nkuni ipempela ubushiku.

Musonda aimba, imyanda yashupa,

Ipilwe yacema, akanya akalola.

Ipilwe ya Musonda, icipe ca lusekelo,

Ubushiku bwakosa, shimuntu shabili shaimba.

Akanya akasanguka, insoni shapwa,

Musonda na Bupe, ku lwa pilwe ya nkuni.

Inkonto ya Bupe (Bupe's Necklace)

Ubushiku bwabuta, inkonto yabuta,

Amatanshi ya Bupe yatemwa icipe.

Icilungamo ca nkuni, icikolwe ca mweemba,

Ipilwe yalenga, inkonto yalengela.

Imfula yashala, inkonto yabila,

Ubushiku bwakosa, umoyo walenga.

Bupe aimba, inkonto yasanguka,

Ipilwe yacema, inkonto yasha.

Inkonto ya Bupe, icipe ca kutemwa,

Ubushiku bwakosa, shimuntu shabili shaimba.

Akanya akasanguka, inkonto yasha,

Bupe na Musonda, ku lwa inkonto ya nkuni.
